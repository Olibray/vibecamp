What is Vibe Coding?

You describe it.
AI builds it.

Vibe coding is the art of building software by talking to AI — describing what you want in plain English and guiding the output into something that actually works. It sounds like magic. It isn't. It's a skill.

The people who get good at it aren't necessarily developers. They're people who think clearly, communicate well, and have the patience to iterate. That's probably you.

In 5 days, you'll go from "I've heard of ChatGPT" to "I just shipped a working product." No CS degree required. No prior coding experience assumed.

What you sound like by Day 5

"Build me a landing page for..."
"Add a waitlist form that emails me when..."
"Make the button only show up when..."
"Deploy this to a live URL so I can..."
"Now make it an agent that automatically..."

These are real prompts. From people who'd never coded before. After 5 days.
